Our innovations

SportsInnovation, Inc. is a leading provider of innovative sports products. Our team of athletes and engineers is dedicated to developing products that help athletes of all levels perform at their best. Our flagship product is the Smart Sports Bra, which features a moisture-wicking, breathable fabric and a unique design that prevents chafing and rubbing. It also includes a built-in heart rate monitor and a series of sensors that track movement and provide real-time feedback on performance. The Smart Sports Bra connects to a smartphone app via Bluetooth, allowing athletes to track their progress and set goals for improvement. In addition to the Smart Sports Bra, we offer a range of other high-tech sports products, including smart running shoes, a smart weightlifting belt, and a smart water bottle. All of our products are priced affordably, and we offer discounts and financing options to make them accessible to athletes of all levels.
